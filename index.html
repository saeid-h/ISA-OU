Who We Are

The University of Oklahoma’s Iranian Student Association, also known as ISA, was established in October of 2005. Iranian Student Association is very fortunate to have such a diverse organization consisting of Iranians who recently moved from Iran, Iranian-Americans, and many other students who are interesting in learning more about Iran’s people and culture. This allows for a wide variety of ideas and opinions, allowing us to improve and enrich our organization.
